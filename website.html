Actor website

Whether you've just graduated from school or a seasoned artist, we're here to help build your website.

Here's how it works:

  • We'll have a first chat about your options (Wix, Squarespace, etc.), their cost, and what you'll need to prepare.
  • You'll tell us about your choice of platform and favorite template, and email us the content you want to see.
  • Fox Theory will build a first version for you, with all the content you've sent us.
  • We'll meet to work on the website together and play with colors, fonts, images, anything you want to try.
  • Fox Theory will put the finishing touches and share the final version.


Pricing

How long will it take?

It can all be very quick if you're fast in doing the "prep work". Depending on availability, it will typically take us less than a week to complete the first version. Then our meeting (IRL or via Skype) will take about 3-4 hours, after which we'll need another day or two to complete last touches.
